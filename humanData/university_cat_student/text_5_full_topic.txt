If you claimed AND scanned your ticket at multiple games and believe you should have early access but have not received the email , PLEASE CHECK YOUR CLUTTER AND JUNK MAIL IF YOU BELIEVE YOU SHOULD GET EARLY ACCESS . The early access period for eligible students will be WEDNESDAY , NOVEMBER 7th from 8:30 am - 4:30 pm . Students who did NOT attend 3 or more of the first 5 games ( via claiming AND scanning student tickets ) will have access to claim student tickets and any remaining guest passes beginning THURSDAY , NOVEMBER 8th at 7 am . If you are a full time student OR have opted in to pay the athletic fee and have issues accessing your account or claiming a ticket , please contact the Adams Center Box Office at 243-4051 OR visit them in the Adams Center M-F , 8:30-4:30 .
